MAINT.Data: Modelling and Analysing Interval Data in R
A. Pedro Duarte Silva, Paula Brito, Peter Filzmoser and José G. Dias

Abstract We present the CRAN R package MAINT.Data for the modelling and analysis of multivariate interval data, i.e., where units are described by variables whose values are intervals of IR, representing intrinsic variability.
