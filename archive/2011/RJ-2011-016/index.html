Nonparametric Goodness-of-Fit Tests for Discrete Null Distributions
Taylor B. Arnold and John W. Emerson , The R Journal (2011) 3:2, pages 34-39.

Abstract Methodology extending nonparametric goodness-of-fit tests to discrete null distributions has existed for several decades. However, modern statistical software has generally failed to provide this methodology to users. We offer a revision of Râ€™s ks.test() function and a new cvm.test() function that fill this need in the R language for two of the most popular nonparametric goodness-of-fit tests. This paper describes these contributions and provides examples of their usage. Particular attention is given to various numerical issues that arise in their implementation.
